Privacy Policy

We care about the safety and return of missing family members.

Missingperson.com.ng will need it's users to register before they can upload a missing person on the platform.

The major user information needed for registeration are: Firstname, lastname, email address, phone number and password. We want to assure you that on no account will any of these details be shared to any third party.

The only detail that will be shared upon the upload of an event by our user will be the phone number of the contact person when there is a hint or the missing person is found.
